The Good and The Bad

Pros

  1. Social media spreads information faster than any other media.
  2. Law enforcement uses social media to catch and prosecute criminals.
  3. The U.S. military and the Department of Veterans Affairs use social media to help prevent suicide.
  4. Social media can help disarm social stigmas like anxiety or depression.
  5. Crowdsourcing on social media allows people to attain a goal, empowering users to achieve positive change.
  6. Social media provides academic research to a wider audience, allowing people access to previously inaccessible educational resources.

Cons

  1. Social media enables the spread of unreliable and false information.
  2. Social media lacks privacy and exposes users to government and corporate intrusions
  3. Social media posts cannot be entirely deleted.
  4. Social media can endanger our military, journalists and activists.
  5. Social media can exacerbate feelings of disconnect and put children at higher risk for anxiety, depression, low self-esteem, eating disorders and even suicide.
